    • Welcome To Coalbot , your dedicated Mining Regulations Assistant. CoalBot is designed to provide quick, accurate, and accessible information on acts, rules, and regulations governing mining operations in India. One of its standout features is the ability to ask questions in the form of images, making it even more convenient for users. Additionally, CoalBot offers voice assistance in multiple languages, ensuring accessibility for a diverse user base. Moreover, you have the option to post a complaint, providing a comprehensive platform for addressing concerns. With a user-friendly interface and regular updates, CoalBot is your go-to resource for navigating the intricacies of mining laws. Simply type your query, upload an image, or use voice commands, and get the information you need, hassle-free. Thank you for choosing CoalBot as your trusted companion in understanding mining regulations in India.


    How To Use Our Coal Bot :


    Using CoalBot is as simple as sending a text message. Just type in your query, and our chatbot will provide you with clear and concise information regarding the relevant acts, rules, and regulations for mining in India. But that's not all - CoalBot goes above and beyond. If you prefer visual communication, you can send us an image related to your query, and CoalBot will analyze it to provide you with accurate and relevant information. Our advanced image recognition capabilities ensure that you receive the right answers, even when words may not suffice.So whether you choose to type, send images, or use your voice, CoalBot is here to assist you every step of the way. Simplifying mining regulations has never been this efficient or convenient. Try it out today and experience the future of mining information at your fingertips.




    CoalBot's Premium Features:

    Image Processing: Users can upload images for analysis. CoalBot converts the image to text, providing relevant information on the data analyzed.

    Voice Assistance: Available in multiple regional languages, allowing users to ask queries effortlessly and provides a best User Experience.

    How CoalBot Helps:

    Mining Regulations: Resolves queries regarding acts, rules, and regulations for the mining industries and Ministry of Coal.
